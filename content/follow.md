Vantage UI is a one-person project, shipping a release every few weeks. There's no newsletter and no marketing list — just the places where the work actually happens. Pick whichever you already live in.

## Where to follow

- **Bluesky — [@nearly.guru](https://bsky.app/profile/nearly.guru)** — release highlights and what's being built, in short.
- **LinkedIn — [Romans Malinovskis](https://www.linkedin.com/in/romansmalinovskis)** — longer notes and the occasional write-up.
- **GitHub — [romaninsh/vantage](https://github.com/romaninsh/vantage)** — the open-source framework and the [example apps](https://github.com/romaninsh/vantage-ui-examples). Star or watch for framework activity. *(The app itself is closed; its releases ship from the Download page.)*

## What's new in the app

Every release is written up for end users. The full, dated changelog — newest first — lives on the **[Download page](/download/)**, synced straight from the app on each release.
